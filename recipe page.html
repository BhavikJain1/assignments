Punjabi Chole



Ingredients :-

  • chickpeas
  • spices
  • chan masala
  • turmeric
  • chilli powder

Step-by-Step Directions :-

  1. Boil chickpeas
  2. Mix it with well cooked masala.
  3. Left it for next 8 min at 100 deg celsius
  4. Now, Your Chole is prepared, served it to guests.
